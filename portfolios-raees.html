Group Reflection:
My role in the group was the group leader, however I did not take this as an opportunity to shy away from doing work and make my team do it all. I did indeed do extensive amount of work on this project. We did work in sessions to break it up and manage time, I had periods in which I worked with Hasan and Habib then when I worked alone. I maintained the group and arranged sessions for us to meet in order to split the work load. This saved us a lot of time and maintained the synergy. I ensured no one slacked on this project and that everyone did their part. At points many of us needed to take breaks due to personal reasons therefore I scheduled breaks between us. I also made us not work for long sessions doing nothing, each session we had was 2 hours maximum in which we went over everything needed. I also ensured that not only did we work as a team but we also did our own work individually in our own times in order for us to work around finishing this project as efficiently as possible. I work on every page in this project spending a lot of times on the foundations and components in the project.
